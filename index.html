what we do

Communication is key to our business. Our highly skilled specialists engage with clients to understand quality specifications. Then through our trusted farmers and local buying agents some of who have over 10 years’ experience in the raw cashew business, we acquire high-quality agricultural commodities for export to Asia.

about us

We are an international trading company focused on the export of agricultural commodities from Nigeria and the import of autos & parts, machineries and equipment. We attentively look in the direction of our customers’ needs and passionately deploy our experience and the expertise of our network of specialists to provide the most satisfactory service.

Core Values: We value integrity, energy and service. We belivein respect for all.

our Strategy

In the long-term, we plan to transition into local processing of commodities so as to create the opportunity for more farmers and unemployed youth to participate in our value chain.
